About Sunshine Animal Sanctuary

Sunshine Animal Sanctuary is a heart-driven shelter located in the heart of the community, where the lives of abandoned and rescued animals are transformed through compassion and care. Founded by a small group of passionate animal lovers, our mission is simple: to provide a safe and loving space for every dog and cat in need while also supporting the people who welcome them into their homes.

Our Team

Our dedicated team is made up of experienced animal caregivers, passionate volunteers, veterinary professionals, and community advocates. Each team member brings their unique skills and unwavering dedication to the mission of saving lives. We're more than coworkers — we're a family united by our love for animals and our belief in second chances.

Helping Animals and Families

We don’t just rescue animals — we change lives. Our adoption program brings hope not only to pets but to the families who welcome them. The bond formed between a rescued animal and a loving family is life-changing and often healing. We’ve seen children grow more confident, seniors find renewed joy, and families find a new sense of togetherness — all thanks to a new furry friend.

Health and Wellness

Every animal that comes to us receives top-tier veterinary care, from emergency treatments to routine vaccinations. Our on-site veterinary team works around the clock to ensure every pet is healthy, happy, and ready for a new home. Wellness is the foundation of our sanctuary, and it’s what gives every adoption story a happy beginning.
