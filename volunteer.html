Become a Volunteer

Volunteering at Sunshine Animal Sanctuary is a life-changing experience—for both you and the animals. Whether it's walking dogs, playing with cats, cleaning habitats, or helping at adoption events, your time helps us create a loving, temporary home for our rescues.

Every volunteer brings something special to the sanctuary. Some bring creativity to design enrichment toys, others bring tech skills to help with our website or social media. Whatever your strengths, there’s a place for you here.

We offer flexible scheduling and welcome students, retirees, and families to get involved. Orientation is provided, and you’ll quickly become part of a passionate team working toward a shared mission: saving lives and spreading sunshine.
